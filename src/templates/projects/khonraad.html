Overview

As Head of Innovation and later Lead Emerging Technologies and AI Architect (2019-2025), I led the innovation initiatives for the Netherlands' primary mental healthcare compliance platform. My role combined maintaining the core platform's architecture while driving innovation projects and ensuring technical excellence across all development activities. I served as second lead during the successful acquisition by Visma in 2020, and represented Khonraad in the expert group ("expertgroep") for the Wvggz-keten organization, where I helped address critical issues in the national care authorization process.

Technical Achievements

Strategic Leadership & Industry Collaboration

I played key roles in both corporate strategy and national healthcare initiatives:

  • Served as second lead during Visma's acquisition of Khonraad, providing comprehensive technical due diligence and documentation
  • Represented Khonraad in the Wvggz-keten expert group, collaborating with stakeholders to improve the national care authorization process
  • Facilitated technical integration discussions between healthcare providers, legal authorities, and IT systems
  • Provided architectural guidance for inter-organizational workflow improvements
  • Contributed to national standards for mental healthcare IT systems

Software Architecture & Documentation

I established and maintained comprehensive architectural documentation following ISO42010 standards:

  • Authored complete architectural documentation covering Context, Functional, Information, Concurrency, Development, and Deployment viewpoints
  • Designed and documented the custom Rule Engine architecture that powers the platform's workflow management
  • Implemented Domain-Driven Design principles to create a shared language between technical and business stakeholders
  • Established architectural guidelines for secure processing of sensitive medical and legal data
  • Created technical documentation that proved instrumental during the company's acquisition by Visma

Legislative Transition & System Evolution

I played a key role in the platform's adaptation to new healthcare legislation:

  • Led technical implementation of new workflows for the transition from BOPZ to WvGGZ/Wzd laws
  • Designed and implemented document generation systems with comprehensive unit testing
  • Enhanced the Rule Engine to support complex new legislative requirements
  • Implemented secure integration points between healthcare providers, legal authorities, and social services
  • Maintained system stability during the transition affecting all 355 Dutch municipalities
